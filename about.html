About T voor Thee

We're a charming bubble tea shop dedicated to bringing joy through every sip! Our teas are made with love and the freshest ingredients, focusing on authentic Taiwanese flavors.

Our Story

Founded in 2025, T voor Thee started as a passion project from a hotel life in Hong Kong to bubble tea dreams in Groningen. We brew real tea, use Taiwanese pearls, and craft each drink with balance, texture, and heart.
